## üéØ Key Questions to Answer Before Designing

Answer these **6 questions** before you start building:

### 1. **Membership**
- Who gets access? (Geography? Stage? Industry?)
- How are they verified? (Manual? Domain? API?)
- What's your trust signal? (Founder status? Batch/cohort?)

### 2. **Content Curation**
- Will you curate content (playbooks, guides)?
- Or just community discussions?
- How do you prevent monoculture?

### 3. **Moderation**
- Who moderates? (You? Community? Combination?)
- What are your rules? (Be explicit‚Äîdocument it.)
- How fast do you respond to spam?

### 4. **Batch/Cohort**
- Do your members have batches/cohorts?
- How do you define them?
- How central are they to identity?

### 5. **Professional Tools**
- Which matter most? (Jobs? Deals? Scheduling? All?)
- MVP first, or all at once?

### 6. **Scale Plan**
- How many members at launch?
- How fast do you grow?
- When do you close/stabilize membership?
